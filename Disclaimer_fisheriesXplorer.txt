3. DATA SPECIFIC INFORMATION 
FisheriesXplorer follows ICES' Fisheries Overviews in providing a summary of fishing activities, fishings impacts and stock status within a
given ICES ecoregion. The data used in each plot and table
is freely available for downloading as a zip file.

Data displayed within FisheriesXplorer are updated with according to different schedules, with some data being updated on annual basis
inline with the stock assessment schedule. Data drawn from ICES stock databases SID, SAG & ASD are continually updated within the app.
Other data sources are updated on an annual or multiannual basis.

Users are urged to treat the data with caution. If the user has any
queries on the validity of the data or the methods applied they should
contact advice@ices.dk.
The published Fisheries Overviews pdfs are the official advice. Though the app and the databases used have been quality controlled, there may be small errors or differences between the published advice and the data in the fisheriesXplorer. If any differences are noticed, please e-mail accessions@ices.dk or luca.lamoni@ices.dk so that corrections can be made.
